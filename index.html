Case Study 01

Modernizing high-volume event management

Case Study 02

Tournament registration made smarter

The existing tournament registration process at Golden Nugget Las Vegas required hours of manual balancing, so I redesigned it and introduced a seating experience that streamlined staff workflows.

Case Study 03

Turning shopping into saving

Stash is an e-commerce experience that turns the act of checking out into an act of saving, helping compulsive shoppers build healthier habits without losing the thrill of checkout.
