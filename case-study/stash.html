Stash

This case study details the design of Stash, a savings and rewards feature, covering onboarding flows, gamification, and data visualization.

Role Product Designer
Team Placeholder
Timeline Placeholder
The problem

Users weren't engaging with savings features despite wanting to save more

Through onboarding data and user interviews, three barriers became clear

Onboarding friction

Setting up savings goals required too many steps, causing most users to drop off before finishing.

Motivation

There was no sense of progress or reward — saving felt like a chore rather than something to look forward to.

Visibility

Users couldn't easily see how their savings were growing over time, making it hard to stay committed.
