Event Discovery

This case study explores the design process behind an event discovery feature, covering user research, interaction design, and visual iteration.

Role Product Designer
Team Placeholder
Timeline Placeholder
The problem

Users struggled to find events that matched their interests

Through user research and interviews, three main pain points emerged

Discoverability

Events were buried in feeds, making it hard for users to find relevant ones without deliberate searching.

Personalization

Recommendations weren't tailored — users saw the same events regardless of their preferences or history.

Context

Event listings lacked key details like distance, mutual friends attending, and social proof.
