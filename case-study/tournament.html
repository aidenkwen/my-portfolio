Tournament Brackets

This case study covers the design of a tournament bracket system, including information architecture, real-time state, and responsive layout.

Role Product Designer
Team Placeholder
Timeline Placeholder
The problem

Managing tournament brackets was confusing and error-prone

After reviewing existing workflows, three core issues stood out

Information architecture

Bracket structures were difficult to parse at a glance, especially as tournaments scaled beyond 16 teams.

Real-time state

Users had no way to see live updates — they had to refresh the page to check if scores or matchups had changed.

Responsive layout

Bracket views were built for desktop and became unusable on mobile, where most users checked results.
